Gilchrist wraps up Round 1

A perfect start to the season in a packed field

Brock Gilchrist got his season off to the perfect start, wrapping up Round 1 to bank the first big haul of championship points.

In a packed grid of SuperMinis the racing was close from lights to flag, but the #10 had the measure of the field when it counted — a result that has him leading the overall standings as the season unfolds.

It is the kind of start that sets the tone for the year, and with a deep field chasing hard there is plenty of racing still to come.
