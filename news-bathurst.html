SuperMinis are going to Bathurst

The SMC takes on the Mountain — 26–28 March 2027

It's official — the SuperMini Challenge is heading to Mount Panorama. The SMC will take on the Hi-Tec Oils Bathurst 6 Hour across 26–28 March 2027, putting a grid of SuperMinis on the most famous stretch of racetrack in the country.

This is an invite-only event for SuperMini members, with limited spots available. Entry is $4,950 including GST, secured with a $2,000 deposit due 25 August 2026 and the balance due 15 January 2027.

The event is backed by major sponsor RubberCraft Tyres Australia, with support from Uptiles Racing, Duce, ABC Building Products, Stormtech and Elliotts.

Spots are expected to go quickly. To secure yours, register your interest today at admin@supermini.au.
