Zane Goddard joins the SuperMini grid

The SuperMini Challenge has landed a big one for 2026, with Zane Goddard bringing his striking purple #83 to the grid.

Resplendent in CLAID and SS Signs colours, Goddard's R53 Cooper S adds serious pace to an already stacked field — and another well-known name to a series that keeps attracting strong drivers without losing its grassroots, run-with-your-mates feel.

It is a great example of what the SuperMini Challenge is all about: evenly matched cars, a packed grid and a friendly paddock that just loves to go racing.

Welcome to the grid, Zane — we can't wait to see the #83 in the mix.
