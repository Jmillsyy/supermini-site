What is the SMC

Grassroots racing, done right.

SuperMini Challenge Inc is a non-profit, Motorsport Australia–affiliated club, run by an elected volunteer committee for the benefit of its members. Since 2022 we've given newcomers and seasoned racers a place to go wheel-to-wheel without breaking the bank.

Two classes, two championships and eight rounds across Queensland, New South Wales and South Australia. A friendly paddock that wants you to succeed.

Cooper Class

BMW R53 Cooper S

The Cooper Class is built on the BMW R53 Cooper S — an evenly matched, affordable car that's exciting to drive and keeps the racing close right through the field. Chasing more pace? The Unlimited Class opens the rulebook for faster, more developed Minis.

Unlimited Class

Built for outright pace

For those chasing outright pace, the Unlimited Class opens the rulebook — built to Motorsport Australia Group 3D Sport Sedan regulations with SMC amendments. Free engine internals, free gearbox and no minimum weight make it the fastest way around the track in a Mini.
