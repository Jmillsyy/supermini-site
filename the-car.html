The BMW R53 Cooper S

The Cooper Class car. Evenly matched. Pure driver versus driver.

The Cooper Class car

Built for close racing

The SuperMini Challenge Cooper Class runs a single, controlled car — the BMW R53 Cooper S. An evenly matched field keeps the racing tight from top to bottom.

The cars are exciting to drive, forgiving enough for newcomers, and rewarding enough to keep experienced racers honest.

After more pace? The Unlimited Class runs alongside Cooper at every round — an open class for faster, more developed Minis. See the rules for what's eligible.

Why a controlled car

The Cooper Class advantage

Closer racing

Evenly matched cars mean tighter packs and more on-track battles all the way down the field.

Controlled cost

A defined platform stops a development arms race, so budgets stay realistic for everyone.

Easy to get started
