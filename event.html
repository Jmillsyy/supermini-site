The circuit

The Bend Motorsport Park

The SuperMini Challenge heads to South Australia for Round 3 of the Rubber Craft Australian Championship at The Bend Motorsport Park, Tailem Bend — one of the longest and most modern circuits in the world.

Both the Cooper and Unlimited classes will be on track across the weekend. Check back here for the full timetable, entry details and the latest event information.
