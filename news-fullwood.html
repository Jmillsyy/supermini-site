Fullwood's grassroots return

Bryce Fullwood is getting back to basics, returning to grassroots racing with the SuperMini Challenge.

For a driver with serious credentials, the appeal is simple: close, evenly matched racing among a friendly paddock, and the pure fun of going wheel-to-wheel.

It is a great endorsement of what the series offers — and another reason to come and watch the SuperMinis go at it.
